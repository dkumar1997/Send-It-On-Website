Sign up

Interested in joining? Visit our sign up page and follow the prompts to begin your SEND IT ON journey

Complete the survey

Once clicking on the sign-up page, you will be directed to a survey. This survey allows SEND IT ON to know your interests, professional background, and availability so that we can better understand your fit within the executive team and/or match you with a mentee in our global communities based on your interests. Within 48 hours of the completion of the survey, SEND IT ON will email you to further discuss the onboarding process.

Onboarding Process

The onboarding process includes:

  • Initial Meeting (virtual)
  • Training Session 2 hrs (virtual)
  • Signing of Documents (virtual/in person)

IF YOU BECOME A MENTOR: Matching Preferences

SEND IT ON will match volunteers with their mentees based on their survey results, initial meeting, and a randomization system. Volunteers will be contacted and a meeting will be set up to discuss the potential mentor/mentee match that has been identified. Communication processes will also be discussed. Here the volunteer will have the chance to ask questions about the mentee and a mutually agreed time will be set to meet the mentee.

Match Meeting

Virtually meet your mentee! Get to know who they are and begin developing a relationship.

Begin to SEND IT ON!

Once the mentor and mentee have connected, the mentor can continue to independently set times with their mentee to meet on a weekly/bi-weekly basis. During this time, the mentor will also be attending SEND IT ON personal growth training sessions which equips them with skills, knowledge, and tools that help them foster and create an environment of change and empowerment. These sessions will also be a place for debrief and process development with our community partners.
