WHAT IS SEND IT ON?

SEND IT ON is a non-profit international organization which aims to empower youth in global communities through mentorship, professional diversity, leadership, entrepreneurship, engagement, and education.

TAKE A GLIMPSE

WHY SEND IT ON?

From mentorship, executive boards, to creating your own project with members in our global communities, SEND IT ON provides a myriad of volunteer opportunities! We help take our mentees and global partners farther than they imagined possible.

OUR GLOBAL PARTNERS

Our global partners are contributing figures within their community who work with youth and citizens to educate and empower.

WHAT IS A MENTOR?

A mentor is a collaborative and curious leader that works with youth around the world to foster interdependency and empower individuals. They have a willingness to create relationships and foster a space for growth, learning, and inspiration.

CELEBRATING OUR FAMILY

SEND IT ON hosts gatherings such as potlucks, bonfires, dinners, and fundraising events to create a sense of community for our family. We strive to be an organization that creates an enjoyable environment while fostering change.
